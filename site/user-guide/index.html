User Guide¶

Welcome to the ShopSync User Guide! This section covers everything you need to know to make the most of ShopSync.

What You'll Learn¶

This guide will teach you how to:

  • Authenticate - Sign in securely with Google or email
  • Manage Lists - Create, organize, and customize shopping lists
  • Work with Items - Add, edit, and organize items effectively
  • Use Categories - Organize items for efficient shopping
  • Share & Collaborate - Work together with family and friends
  • Work Offline - Use ShopSync without internet connection
  • Customize Settings - Personalize your ShopSync experience
