    WearOS Support¶

    ShopSync brings your shopping lists to your wrist with full WearOS support.

    Features¶

    Quick View¶

    Glance at your shopping list without pulling out your phone:

    • View all unchecked items
    • See item quantities and notes
    • Scroll through multiple lists
    • Check off items with a tap

    Voice Commands¶

    Add items hands-free:

    Voice Commands" - "Add milk to grocery list" - "Check off bread" - "Show my shopping list" - "Mark all as bought

    Complications¶

    Add ShopSync to your watch face:

    • Short text: Number of unchecked items
    • Long text: Next item on list
    • Icon: Quick launch to app
    • Ranged value: Shopping progress

    Installation¶

    1. Install ShopSync on your Android phone
    2. Open Play Store on your watch
    3. Search for ShopSync
    4. Tap Install
    5. Sign in with same account

    Using the Watch App¶

    Navigation¶

    • Swipe left/right: Switch between lists
    • Swipe up: Scroll through items
    • Tap item: Complete/uncomplete item
    • Long press: See item details (read-only)
    • Press crown: Return to home

    Editing Restrictions

    You cannot create, edit, or delete items from the watch. All editing must be done on your phone or web app.

    Syncing¶

    The watch app syncs automatically:

    • Every 30 seconds when app is open
    • On app launch
    • When you complete/uncomplete an item
    • When phone app makes changes

    Battery Impact

    WearOS app is optimized for battery life. Syncing only occurs when needed.

    Offline Mode¶

    The watch app stores your lists locally:

    • View lists without phone connection
    • Complete/uncomplete items offline
    • Changes sync when connection restored
    • Cache cleared after 7 days

    Offline Limitations

    Only completion status can be changed offline. Creating or editing items requires connection to your phone or web app.

    Customization¶

    Watch Face Integration¶

    Add complications to your watch face:

    1. Long-press your watch face
    2. Tap Customize
    3. Select complication slot
    4. Choose ShopSync
    5. Select complication type

    Tile Support¶

    Quick access via tiles:

    1. Swipe left from watch face
    2. Tap Add tile
    3. Select ShopSync
    4. Configure display options

    Troubleshooting¶

    App Not Syncing¶

    1. Ensure phone and watch are connected
    2. Open ShopSync on phone
    3. Go to Settings â†’ WearOS
    4. Tap Force Sync

    Can't See Lists¶

    • Check that lists aren't archived
    • Verify you're signed in
    • Ensure phone app is updated
    • Try reinstalling watch app

    Voice Commands Not Working¶

    1. Enable voice permissions for ShopSync
    2. Check Google Assistant is working
    3. Use clear, simple commands
    4. Ensure phone is nearby

    !!! tip "Battery Optimization" - Close app when not in use - Disable always-on display - Reduce sync frequency in settings

    Supported Watches¶

    ShopSync works on all WearOS 2.0+ devices:

    • Samsung Galaxy Watch⅘/6
    • TicWatch Pro ¾/5
    • Fossil Gen 6
    • Mobvoi watches
    • Google Pixel Watch

    See also: Offline Mode | Settings
